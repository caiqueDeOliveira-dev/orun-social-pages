Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Orun Social is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use our services.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Orun Social does not collect, store, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Cookies or tracking technologies
  • Data from other users
  • Content from your social media feeds

3. How the Application Works

The application connects directly to your own social media accounts using official OAuth authentication. All data flows directly between your device and the social media platforms' APIs. No data passes through our servers.

4. Data Storage

All data generated by the application (posts, schedules, settings) is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers or cloud services.

5. Third-Party Services

The application interfaces with the following third-party platforms through their official APIs:

  • Instagram (Meta) - for posting content to your own account
  • TikTok - for posting content to your own account
  • X (Twitter) - for posting content to your own account

Your use of these platforms is subject to their respective privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
